Telecommunication Bill 2023: Key Analysis

1. Major Provisions

  • ● Replaces colonial-era laws (Indian Telegraph Act 1885)
  • ● Administrative allocation for satellite spectrum
  • ● Biometric KYC mandate for SIM cards
  • ● Digital Bharat Nidhi (₹4,000 cr rural connectivity fund)

2. Controversial Aspects

  • ● Section 20: Govt surveillance without judicial oversight
  • ● Ambiguous OTT regulation (WhatsApp/Zoom inclusion?)
  • ● Bypasses TRAI recommendations in "public interest"

4. Key Statistics

  • ● ₹12.5L cr: Size of Indian telecom sector (2023)
  • ● 100% 5G coverage target: 2030
  • ● ₹35,000 cr: Estimated telco savings from spectrum reforms

5. Way Forward

  • ● Judicial oversight for surveillance (High Court approval)
  • ● Exclude OTTs from licensing requirements
  • ● 1% AGR allocation for 6G R&D
